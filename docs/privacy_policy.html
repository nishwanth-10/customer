🔒 Privacy Policy

Customer Ledger Pro

Last updated: May 2026

Welcome to Customer Ledger Pro. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and phone number when you register.
  • Business Data: Customer names, mobile numbers, payment amounts, and transaction records you enter into the app.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and FCM token for push notifications.
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns for improving the service (anonymized).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Customer Ledger Pro service.
  • To send payment reminders to your customers via SMS, WhatsApp, or push notifications (only with your explicit action).
  • To authenticate you and maintain your session securely.
  • To send you important service updates and security alerts.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Twilio: For sending SMS and WhatsApp reminders (only mobile numbers you provide).
  • Firebase (Google): For push notifications and authentication.
  • Our hosting provider (Render/Railway): For server infrastructure.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in encrypted PostgreSQL databases.
  • API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Authentication tokens are stored using Android Keystore-backed secure storage.
  • We implement rate limiting and input validation to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Customer Data You Enter

The customer records you create (names, numbers, payment data) are your data. You are responsible for ensuring you have appropriate consent from your customers to store and process their information.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Export your data in machine-readable format.

8. Children's Privacy

Customer Ledger Pro is intended for business owners and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
